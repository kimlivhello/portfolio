Hello! I'm a developer that loves to create clean & intuitive websites.

A little bit about me.

I'm a research-psychologist-turned-coder based in New England. As the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I took the opportunity to switch careers. I decided to start my own website devoted to sharing the content that I already taught in my college classes. Rather than relying on an out-of-the-box predesigned site, I wanted to learn how to build my own home on the internet to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my understanding of human psychology and my organization skills and eye for detail to create intuitive, beautiful websites.
